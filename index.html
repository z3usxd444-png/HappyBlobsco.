Guidelines + Care

Helpful notes for running a slime shop + safety reminders for customers.

Slime care basics

  • Wash hands before and after play.
  • Store slime in a closed container to keep it fresh.
  • If slime feels sticky, use a tiny bit of activator (follow your care card).
  • Keep away from fabric, carpet, and hair.

Safety notes

  • Not edible ‚Äî do not eat.
  • Ages 6+ recommended, with adult help for younger kids.
  • Stop use if skin irritation happens. Wash with soap and water.
  • Some recipes may include borax/contact-solution activator.

How do I take care of my slime?

Keep it in its container, play with clean hands, and keep it away from fabric and hair. If it gets sticky, use a tiny bit of activator.

Is slime safe?

Slime is generally safe when used correctly, but it is not edible. Ages 6+ recommended and stop using if irritation happens.
